Tune Lab — Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Tune Lab is a guitar tuner app developed by VAN ANH AUDIO COMPANY LIMITED. This policy explains how the app handles your information.

Data Collection

Tune Lab does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. The app does not require an account, does not track usage, and does not use analytics or advertising services.

Microphone Access

The app requests access to your device's microphone solely to detect pitch in real time. Audio is processed locally on your device and is never recorded, stored, or sent anywhere.

Third-Party Services

Tune Lab does not integrate any third-party SDKs, analytics tools, or advertising networks.

Children's Privacy

Tune Lab does not collect any information from users of any age.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this URL.
