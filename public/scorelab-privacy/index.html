ScoreLab Trainer — Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

ScoreLab Trainer is a music note-reading practice app developed by VAN ANH AUDIO COMPANY LIMITED. This policy explains how the app handles your information.

Data Collection

ScoreLab Trainer does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. The app does not require an account, does not track usage, and does not use analytics or advertising services.

Game Progress

Your level progress and best scores are saved locally on your device only. This information never leaves your device and is not sent to us or any third party. Removing the app deletes this data.

Third-Party Services

ScoreLab Trainer does not integrate any third-party SDKs, analytics tools, or advertising networks.

Children's Privacy

ScoreLab Trainer does not collect any information from users of any age.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this URL.
